About GenAI Academy

Where Innovation Meets Education

GenAI Academy is a forward-thinking educational platform dedicated to making Generative AI accessible to everyone. Founded by passionate young developers from Sri Lanka, we believe that the future of technology lies in AI — and that future starts here.

Our academy offers comprehensive courses in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, and Generative AI. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced developer looking to upskill, GenAI Academy provides the knowledge, tools, and community you need to succeed.

We combine theoretical foundations with practical, hands-on projects so that our students don't just learn AI — they build with it. Our curriculum is constantly updated to reflect the latest advancements in the rapidly evolving AI landscape.
